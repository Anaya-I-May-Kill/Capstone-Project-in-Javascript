My Favourite Anime





Haikyuu - Spike Up Your Life: The Underdog Story You Didn’t Know You Needed

Alright, listen up. Haikyuu! isn’t just some sports anime, it’s the sports anime. You think you’re ready for a team of underdogs who don’t even know what they’re doing at first but somehow end up breaking every limit they have? This show is an emotional rollercoaster of heart, sweat, and insane volleyball moves that'll have you cheering like a madman. It’s about passion, teamwork, and realizing that hard work is the ultimate game-changer. Trust me, you’ll be screaming "Karasuno!" at your screen and jumping out of your seat when they pull off the most ridiculous plays. Who knew volleyball could make you feel like you just ran a marathon?





Jujutsu Kaisen - Get Cursed: The Battle Between Humans and Monsters Has Never Been This Intense

Jujutsu Kaisen is that show that takes everything you thought you knew about shonen anime, flips it upside down, and punches it in the face. You got cursed spirits, insane powers, and a main character who's basically a walking disaster but in the best way. The action? Next level. The stakes? Higher than your last-minute panic during finals. And don’t even get me started on the characters—everyone from Gojo to Fushiguro is so badass you’ll forget they’re even fictional. This anime doesn’t waste time with fluff; it just hits you with one epic fight after another. Get ready to question your own will to live, because this one? It’s a wild ride that’ll have you hooked from episode one.





Blue Lock - No Mercy on the Field: Soccer, but with Savage Ambition

Forget about the world’s most boring soccer teams. Blue Lock takes soccer to a whole new level of savage. This isn’t just about kicking a ball around—it’s about becoming the ultimate striker, and no one’s here to play nice. A whole bunch of the best players are thrown into a crazy, high-stakes competition where only one can come out on top. It’s full of ruthless tactics, brutal elimination, and characters who will do anything to win. If you thought soccer was chill, Blue Lock will teach you that it’s really just a battlefield disguised as a sport. And you? You’ll be screaming at the screen as you get caught up in the drama, the rivalries, and the insane goals.
